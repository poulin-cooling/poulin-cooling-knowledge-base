## Service Overview

Poulin Cooling provides expert air conditioning repair for residential and commercial properties throughout Clearwater and Pinellas County. We diagnose and fix all AC problems quickly, with same-day service available.

## Common AC Problems We Fix

- AC not cooling or blowing warm air
- AC not turning on
- Frozen evaporator coils
- Refrigerant leaks
- Compressor failures
- Faulty capacitors and contactors
- Thermostat malfunctions
- Unusual noises (banging, squealing, rattling)
- Water leaks or excessive condensation
- Poor airflow or weak air circulation
