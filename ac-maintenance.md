## Service Overview

Regular AC maintenance extends system life, improves energy efficiency, and prevents unexpected breakdowns. Poulin Cooling offers seasonal tune-ups and maintenance plans for residential and commercial customers.

## What's Included in a Tune-Up

- Inspect and clean evaporator and condenser coils
- Check refrigerant levels
- Inspect electrical connections and components
- Lubricate moving parts
- Check thermostat calibration
- Inspect air filters and ductwork
- Test system performance and airflow

## Recommended Frequency

At least once per year, ideally before the cooling season (spring). Florida's year-round heat makes twice-yearly maintenance ideal.
