## Air Conditioning

**My AC is not cooling — what should I do?**
Call us at (727) 424-2222. Common causes include low refrigerant, a dirty air filter, frozen coils, or a faulty compressor. We offer same-day diagnosis and repair.

**How often should I have my AC serviced?**
At least once a year, ideally before the cooling season begins. Regular maintenance extends equipment life and prevents costly breakdowns.

**How long does an AC system last?**
Most central AC systems last 12–15 years with proper maintenance. If your system is over 10 years old and needs frequent repairs, replacement may be more cost-effective.

**Do you install ductless mini-split systems?**
Yes. We install ductless mini-split and multi-zone systems for homes and businesses without existing ductwork.

## Indoor Air Quality

**What indoor air quality services do you offer?**
We install and service air purifiers, UV germicidal lights, HEPA filtration systems, whole-home dehumidifiers, and ventilation upgrades to improve the air you breathe indoors.

**How do I know if I have an indoor air quality problem?**
Signs include persistent allergies, musty odors, excessive dust, humidity issues, or respiratory irritation indoors. We can assess your home or business and recommend solutions.
