## Service Overview

Annual heating maintenance keeps your system running safely and efficiently. Poulin Cooling offers heating tune-ups for homes and businesses in Pinellas County.

## What's Included

- Inspect heat exchanger (gas systems)
- Check electrical connections and components
- Test thermostat operation
- Inspect air filters
- Check refrigerant levels (heat pumps)
- Test safety controls
- Verify proper airflow
